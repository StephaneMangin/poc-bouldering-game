# MVP Validation Criteria

## Gameplay
1. 80% of players understand the basic controls in < 2 min.
2. Median run duration between 5 and 15 min.
3. 70% of players voluntarily restart at least one run.

## Readability
1. 70% of players correctly explain the cause of their last failure.
2. 80% identify hold quality via UI/UX feedback.
3. Less than 20% of failures are judged arbitrary.

## Balancing
1. Completion: Easy > Medium > Hard.
2. Arcade completions >= Simulation on same route.
3. No single system (chalk/focus) dominates all strategies.

## UX and Accessibility
1. Keyboard rebinding successful for >= 95% of attempts.
2. Feedback intensity options reduce reported visual discomfort.

## Stability
1. No blocking crash during targeted playtests.
2. Critical bug rate < 1 per 30 minutes of session.

## Go / No-Go Decision
1. `Go`: 80% of criteria met, no critical blocker.
2. `Conditional Go`: 60-79% met with 1-sprint correction plan.
3. `No-Go`: < 60% met or unresolved critical bug.
