# Design Risks

## R1: Control Perceived as "Stiff"
1. Probability: medium.
2. Impact: high.
3. Mitigation: early playtests + adjustment of input/camera curves.

## R2: Overloaded Feedback
1. Probability: medium.
2. Impact: medium.
3. Mitigation: UI/UX intensity levels + prioritization of critical alerts.

## R3: Unfair Difficulty Perceived
1. Probability: high.
2. Impact: high.
3. Mitigation: strengthen feedback causality and reduce opaque punishments.

## R4: Repetitive Run Loop
1. Probability: medium.
2. Impact: medium.
3. Mitigation: route variants and differentiated score objectives.

## R5: Too Slow Progression
1. Probability: medium.
2. Impact: medium.
3. Mitigation: calibrate XP for first significant unlock in 60-120 min.

## R6: Technique Overload for New Players
1. Probability: high.
2. Impact: high.
3. Mitigation: progressive unlock system (L1-L10) gates techniques. Tutorial introduces only basic grabs. Advanced techniques unlocked with clear explanation.

## R7: Physics Model Feels "Wrong"
1. Probability: medium.
2. Impact: high.
3. Mitigation: reference real climbing videos for COG behavior. Iterative tuning with climber feedback. Arcade mode softens physics penalties.

## R8: Audio Breaks Immersion
1. Probability: low.
2. Impact: medium.
3. Mitigation: spatial audio testing, volume mixing priority system, optional mute per layer.

## R9: Wall Angle Transition Feels Jarring
1. Probability: medium.
2. Impact: medium.
3. Mitigation: smooth interpolation of angle multipliers at panel transitions. Visual cues (panel seams) telegraph angle changes.
